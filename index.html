About Me

I was brought up in the Philippines, away from the technologies and the lifestyle that cuture shocked me when I arrived in California when I was 16. Albeit the initial frustrations, I have come to love living in America. It was around this time that I was given my own computer. A hand me down from one of my cousins. It was then that I learned delete didn't simply close an application rather, it erased it. It was then where I learned more about how a computer works and all the great things you can accomplish with it.

During my time at SolarCity, I performed most of my work using our own internal programs and as a new and upcoming company some of the applications that were used had some growing pains. I cannot remember how many tickets we submitted to our Software team. In time, through collaboration and team work we were able to amend these growing pains. It was one thing to be able to point out some things that didn't make sense anymore and one thing to be able to do something about the problem. Hence, I enrolled through Iron Yard to reacquiant myself with programming and I've loved the technology ever since.
